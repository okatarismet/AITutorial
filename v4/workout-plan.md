# Workout Plan — Upper / Lower (4 Days)

**Schedule:** Mon = Upper A · Tue = Lower A · Thu = Upper B · Fri = Lower B
(Wed / Sat / Sun = rest. Each muscle is trained 2× per week.)

**Rules every session:**
- Take every working set to **0–3 reps from failure (RIR)**.
- **Double progression:** stay at the same weight until you hit the TOP of the rep range
  on all sets, then add 2.5–5 lb and drop back to the bottom of the range.
- Rest: **2–3 min** on compounds, **60–90 sec** on isolations.
- Warm up: 2–3 light ramp-up sets on the first compound of the day (not counted as working sets).
- **Log every set** (weight × reps) in the column provided.

## Progression & Recovery Reminders
- **Beat the logbook every week** — one more rep or 2.5–5 lb. That's the whole game.
- **Add a set** to a lagging muscle every 2–3 weeks until you reach your volume ceiling, then hold.
- **Deload every 6–10 weeks:** cut volume ~40–50% for one week when joints ache or weights stall.
- **Protein:** ~0.7–1 g/lb bodyweight daily. **Sleep:** 7–9 h. Slight surplus (+200–300 kcal) to grow.

> Find your starting weights yourself: pick a load you can do for the BOTTOM of the rep
> range while leaving 2–3 reps in the tank. If you blow past the top of the range easily,
> it's too light — go up next session.
